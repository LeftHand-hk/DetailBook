# Auto Detailing SaaS Platform - Project Context

## Overview
B2B SaaS platform targeting auto detailing businesses in the USA. Niche-focused solution differentiating from generic tools like Square, Vagaro, and Booksy.

## Core Features (V1)
- Online booking system
- Calendar/scheduling
- Payment processing
- Analytics dashboard
- Vehicle-type booking (sedan, SUV, truck, etc.)
- Automatic service duration calculation
- Mandatory deposit on booking
- Before/after photo upload

## Pricing
- **Starter:** $29/month
  - Core booking & scheduling
  - Payment processing
  - Analytics
  - Vehicle-type booking
  - Before/after photos
- **Pro:** $50/month
  - Everything in Starter, plus:
  - SMS reminders
  - Priority support
  - Multiple staff/calendars
  - Custom branding
- **Grandfather pricing** offered to early customers

## V2 Features (Deferred until 20-30 clients)
- Custom branding with subdomain routing
- Dynamic CSS variables for client themes

## Go-to-Market Strategy
- Meta Ads with fixed monthly budget
- 50% of monthly profit reinvested into ads
- First 5-10 businesses get free trial period for feedback and testimonials
- Cold email/DMs running in parallel with ads at launch

## Competitive Positioning
- Niche-focused on auto detailing (not a generic booking tool)
- Competitors: Square, Vagaro, Booksy (all generic, not tailored for detailing)

## Key Decisions
- No manual outreach preferred; focus on paid ads
- Custom branding deferred to V2 to keep V1 lean
- Mandatory deposit to reduce no-shows
- Before/after photos as a differentiator for detailing businesses
- Multi-tenant SaaS (each detailing business gets their own account)
